🌴 Rules & Regulations 🌴

To ensure safety, comfort, and enjoyment for all guests, please strictly follow these rules:

👙 Proper Swimwear

• Wear appropriate swimwear (swimsuits, rashguards, swimming trunks, cycling shorts, etc.)

• Maong/denim shorts, clothing with buttons and zippers are not allowed in the pool.

• Babies must wear swimming diapers.

🚿 Shower Before Swimming

• All guests must shower before entering the pool.

• Guests with open wounds, skin infections, or contagious illness are not allowed to swim.

👶 Children Supervision

• Children 12 years old and below must be accompanied and supervised by an adult at all times.

• Lifeguards are for emergency response only; parental supervision is still required.

⚠️ Safety Rules

• No running around pool areas.

• No diving in shallow areas.

• No rough play, pushing, or dangerous behavior.

• No glass bottles or breakable items near the pool.

• No eating inside the pool area.

• No smoking inside the pool area.

📋 Resort Responsibility

• Swim at your own risk.

• Management is not liable for lost belongings or accidents due to failure to follow rules.
